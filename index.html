About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher specializing in Nonlinear Dynamics, Vibrational Control, and Fluid-Structure Interaction. My work blends complex theoretical modeling with experimental validation to navigate and control bifurcations in engineering systems.

Current Focus: Developing a comprehensive research proposal for the Newton International Fellowship on the topic of NV center diamond MEMS, aiming to push the boundaries of sensing and quantum-mechanical applications in micro-electromechanical systems.
